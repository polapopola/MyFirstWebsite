How It Works

Using our non-invasive device, we measure the microcirculation under your tongue, providing a snapshot of your overall vascular health.

Analyze

Our advanced software analyzes the data, identifying signs of endothelial dysfunction and other microvascular issues.

Recommend

Receive personalized health insights and actionable recommendations from our network of health experts.

Improve

Implement lifestyle changes and treatments tailored to your unique needs, and track your progress over time for continuous improvement.
